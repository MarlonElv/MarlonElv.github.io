Quality Management Overview

Project Quality Issues & Resolution: Quality remained a major issue during the Easely development. After John Ruiz left, there was an entire month without quality guidance. The regional quality manager was subsequently fired, with no replacement available until February 2026. This created significant challenges with documentation turn over, client FIA submissions, tooling certification, and more. The impact of incomplete torque checks was estimated at $150,000 in rework due to the absence of strong quality management oversight and relationships with IPX. Resolution involved establishing pile remediation logs, leading to an expensive concrete collar remediation to substitute pull testing. Financial impact and disrupted progress were documented throughout the project lifecycle.
